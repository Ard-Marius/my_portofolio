The Science of Stones and Stories.


My educational journey commenced with a profound fascination for the Earth, particularly in the study of rocks and minerals. Through the discipline of geology, I uncovered the intricate processes that mold our planet, fostering my creative spirit and igniting a passion for meticulous attention to detail and design. The delicate equilibrium of nature’s complexity and simplicity has profoundly influenced my worldview, guiding my approach to challenges and visual composition in all my projects.




From Earth’s Layers to Coding Layers.


Stepping into the world of coding has been an exciting new chapter, blending my passion for detail and design with the dynamic field of front-end development. As a junior front-end developer, I’m dedicated to building clean, engaging user interfaces that not only function seamlessly but also resonate with users. Each project is an opportunity to learn and refine my skills, integrating creativity with coding to transform conceptual ideas into functional web-based experiences.
